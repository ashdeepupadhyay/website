BIO

Hi! I am Ashdeep Upadhyay. I work at Ticketmaster on research projects, writing python scripts to help designers and making a tool for creating maps. I am a passionate developer and I take pride in the multiple delivered projects I have worked on and the teams I have worked with. I studied Engineering(Information Technology) from the Army Institute Of Technology. I did just competitive programming for initial years in college and then started with web development(build a various website for college fest , admissions etc) and later did my final year project building a pipeline for a company Calsoft. After passing out of college joined the company Livelike where I learned a lot and we started with around eight people and grew to 35 in India only(Livelike have an office in US and France too). From doing French Open , FIFA 2018 and various other projects it was a dream come too (Sports is one of the domains which I always love) and roller coaster journey while making some bonds hopefully for life. I like to write blogs , travel , make food, learn a different programming language , technologies and make a small prototype using them. I consider myself a 'forever student', eager to learn new tech stacks everyday.
